I'm the Director of Reseach at Sunbird AI in Uganda. Previously I was a Staff Research Software Engineer at Google Research, and tech lead for Africa projects at UN Global Pulse.

Some projects:

  • Sunflower is a collection of LLMs that support 31 Ugandan languages, optimised for machine translation.
  • I started the Open Buildings project in Google Brain, using computer vision and satellite imagery to map (nearly) all the buildings in Africa, Latin America and South/Southeast Asia.
  • At the UN I worked on language technology for early warning from community radio stations.
